Throughout my internship, I had the opportunity to explore and work with a wide range of services on the Amazon Web Services (AWS) platform while actively participating in the development and deployment of the **SmartStudy AI** project. This internship enabled me to apply the knowledge I had learned at university to real-world scenarios and gain hands-on experience in developing and deploying cloud-based applications.

Through the project, I gained practical experience with AWS services such as AWS Amplify, Amazon Cognito, Amazon API Gateway, AWS Lambda, Amazon S3, Amazon DynamoDB, Amazon SQS, and Amazon CloudWatch. In addition, I had the opportunity to study serverless architecture, infrastructure deployment using AWS CloudFormation and AWS CDK, as well as integrating a locally hosted AI model into the system through Cloudflare Relay.

During the internship, I consistently strived to complete assigned tasks on schedule, proactively studied the official AWS documentation, and collaborated with team members whenever I encountered challenges. This experience not only strengthened my technical knowledge but also improved my teamwork, self-learning, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills in a professional working environment.

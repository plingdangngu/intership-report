> This section summarizes my personal experience throughout the First Cloud AI Journey program and provides suggestions that may help improve the learning experience for future participants.

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Learning Environment

The learning environment was well organized, supportive, and highly motivating. The program provided a clear learning roadmap that gradually introduced AWS services before moving on to real project implementation. The combination of self-learning materials, workshops, and hands-on practice created an effective learning experience.

### 2. Mentor and Community Support

The mentors and community members were always willing to answer questions and provide guidance whenever difficulties arose. Instead of simply giving solutions, they encouraged participants to analyze problems independently and explore the AWS documentation. This approach helped me become more confident in troubleshooting and problem-solving.

### 3. Relevance to My Academic Background

The program was closely aligned with my university major in Information Technology. It allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge in software development while learning practical cloud computing concepts, serverless architecture, and AWS best practices that are not always covered in university courses.

### 4. Learning Opportunities

One of the most valuable aspects of the program was the opportunity to build a complete cloud application instead of only completing isolated labs. Developing the SmartStudy AI project allowed me to gain practical experience with AWS Amplify, Amazon Cognito, API Gateway, Lambda, S3, DynamoDB, SQS, CloudWatch, CloudFormation, and AWS CDK while also improving my documentation and technical writing skills.

### 5. Community and Collaboration

The program fostered a collaborative atmosphere where participants were encouraged to share ideas and help one another. Discussions within the community made learning more engaging and allowed me to learn from the experiences of other participants.

### 6. Overall Program Experience

Overall, I believe the First Cloud AI Journey program successfully bridges the gap between academic learning and practical cloud development. The combination of guided learning, real projects, technical blogs, and workshops provided a comprehensive learning experience that strengthened both my technical knowledge and practical skills.

## Additional Questions

### What was the most satisfying part of the program?

The most rewarding experience was building and deploying the SmartStudy AI project on AWS. Seeing the application evolve from an initial idea into a working cloud-based system gave me a deeper understanding of AWS services and increased my confidence in designing and deploying cloud applications.

### What could be improved?

It would be beneficial to include more advanced workshops focusing on topics such as CI/CD, Infrastructure as Code, cloud security best practices, and cost optimization. Additional sessions covering troubleshooting techniques and real-world deployment scenarios would also help participants gain a deeper understanding of production environments.

### Would you recommend this program to your friends?

Yes. I would definitely recommend the First Cloud AI Journey program to students who want to learn AWS through practical experience. The program provides a structured learning path, opportunities to work on real projects, and valuable guidance from experienced mentors, making it an excellent starting point for anyone interested in cloud computing.

## Suggestions and Expectations

- Continue updating the learning materials to include newly released AWS services and features.
- Organize additional technical sharing sessions where participants can present their projects and exchange experiences.
- Provide more real-world project scenarios that simulate enterprise environments.
- Include optional advanced learning tracks for participants who want to explore cloud architecture, DevOps, AI, or security in greater depth.
- Maintain and further expand the supportive learning community, as it is one of the program's greatest strengths.

Overall, I am grateful for the opportunity to participate in the First Cloud AI Journey program. The knowledge, practical experience, and confidence I gained throughout the program will serve as a strong foundation for my future career in cloud computing and software engineering.
